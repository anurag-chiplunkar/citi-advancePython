Statistical Analysis: set of techniques and methods to interpret and draw meaningful consulsion from data.



set of techniques and methods:
1. data collection: it has to be meaningful, it should give relevant data
2. descripitive statistics: mean, median, mode, variability
3. inferential statistics: making inferences or predictions about a population
4. hypothesis testing: formulating statement
5. probability: just to check the uncertinity
6. sampling techniques: choosing right technique
7. statistical test: to check the output with standard output
8. data visualization: graphical representation of data
9. interpretation and conclusion: result/ prediction
